Recognition Test

This test aims to assess your ability to distinguish between visually similar Chinese radicals. Your results will help improve teaching methods.

You will see 30 questions, each question asks you to identify the correct Chinese radical, the options will appear 5 seconds after the questions be shown, choose the BEST & ONLY answer from options A to D.

You will have 10s to answer each question. Once you answer the question, you will Not go back to check your answer.

You will have 20 minutes to finish this test.
